The Finals: A New Era of Esports

The Finals is revolutionizing the FPS genre with fully destructible environments, creating unparalleled strategic depth and a thrilling spectator experience.

The competitive scene is rapidly expanding, highlighted by an official Major in 2025 with a $100,000 prize pool. Qualifiers begin this August, culminating in a live final at DreamHack Stockholm.

The entry of major organizations like Spacestation Gaming (SSG) and OpTic Gaming signifies the game's immense potential, attracting top-tier talent and accelerating the growth of the entire ecosystem.

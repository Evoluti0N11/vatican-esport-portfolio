The Finals: A New Era of Esports

The Finals is redefining the FPS genre with its unique, fully destructible arenas, creating unparalleled strategic depth and spectator engagement.

The competitive scene is expanding rapidly, highlighted by an official Major in 2025 with a $100,000 prize pool. Open qualifiers in August lead to a grand final at DreamHack Stockholm, marking a significant milestone for the esport.

Furthermore, the entry of major organizations like Space Station Gaming (SSG) and Envy validates the game's potential as a premier esport, attracting top-tier talent and boosting viewership, signaling a vibrant future for the entire competitive ecosystem.
